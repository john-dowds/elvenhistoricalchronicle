The Chronicle Side Car

The Chronicle Side Car feature allows users to work on filling out their character’s application side by side with the latest editions of the Chronicle, allowing a good frame of reference when needed. Be sure you are logged into your email account, otherwise it may not save.

Warning: Clearing your browser cache or history may risk erasing your progress if you haven’t submitted.
